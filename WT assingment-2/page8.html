3. Explore celestial bodies in a virtual environment ?

  • 1.Lunar Surface Exploration : Users can explore the Moon’s surface using virtual reality (VR) or augmented reality(AR) technology. They can visit landing sites of apollo missions, walk or drive the lunar rover , and experience the moon’s gravity and terrain firsthand.

  • 2.Virtual Lunar : Users can pilot virtual lunar modules , simulating the descent and ascent stages of Apollo missions . They can control the spacecraft’s thrusters , manage fuel consumption , and execute precision landings on designated lunar sites.

  • 3.Scientific Experiments : Users can conduct virtual experiments on the Moon’s surface, similar to those performed by Apollo astronauts .They can deploy scientific instruments , collect lunar samples ,take photographs , and analyze geological features .

  • 4.Apollo Mission Reconstruction : Users can participate in reconstructed Apollo missions, following historical timelines and objectives . they can experience key moments such as lunar module separation , moonwalks , and lunar liftoff.
